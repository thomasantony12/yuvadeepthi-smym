About Yuvadeepti

The founder of this Organization is His Grace Mar Joseph Powathil. Yuvadeepti-SMYM is the youth apostolate of Changanacherry Archdiocese. Yuvadeepti is a part of KCYM (Kerala Catholic Youth Movement), which is affiliated to ICYM (Indian Catholic Youth Movement). As the name suggests Yuvadeepti is national wide catholic youth organization which is aiming at the integral development of every youth. The word “Yuvadeepti” is joined by two words “Yuva” and “Deepti”. “Yuva” means youth and “Deepti” means light. Yes we are to give the light of Jesus to our youth.

Where

The movement was inaugurated by Mar. Antony Padiyara at Kallarackal Hall, S. B.College Changanacherry.

When

The Youth Apostolate "YUVADEEPTI" of the Archdiocese of Changanacherry was established on 3rd December 1972.
